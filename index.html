Brand Story

Berlin-brewed.
Korea-inspired. 베를린에서 빚고, 한국에서 영감 받았다

Two cultures don't always mix easily. Germany and Korea have little in common on the surface. Different language, different food, a different way of living.

But sit down together with good food and a drink, and that distance shrinks a little. Soolgom started from that simple idea. Brewed in Germany, inspired by Korean tradition, a drink that bridges the two.
